Psychiatry & Mental Health Services

  1. Home
  2. Specialities
  3. Psychiatry & Mental Health Services

The Psychiatry Department at Pasunooti Neuro and Multispeciality Care provides comprehensive mental health care with a patient-centered approach. Our expert psychiatrists specialize in diagnosing, treating, and managing various psychiatric disorders, ensuring holistic well-being for all age groups.

Key Services & Expertise

    Comprehensive Mental Health Care

  • Treatment for anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and other mood disorders.
  • Child and adolescent mental health services for emotional and behavioral issues.
  • Geriatric mental health care for conditions like dementia and late-life depression.
  • De-addiction therapy for individuals struggling with substance use disorders.
  • Rehabilitation for child and adolescent learning disabilities.
  • Rehabilitation for geriatric problems.

    • Personalized Treatment Approaches

    Each patient receives an individualized care plan, including:

  • Psychotherapy (CBT, mindfulness therapy, behavioral therapy, and parent management therapy).
  • Medication management for effective treatment outcomes.
  • Counseling and lifestyle guidance for long-term mental well-being.

    • State-of-the-Art Facilities & Support

  • OPD and inpatient services for comprehensive psychiatric care.
  • Day care programs for structured mental health support.
  • EEG and advanced neuropsychiatric assessments for accurate diagnosis.
  • Crisis intervention services for psychiatric emergencies.
  • Family counseling, marital counseling, and career guidance for holistic recovery.

Holistic Approach to Mental Wellness

We focus on treating the person, not just the condition, addressing mental, emotional, and social factors to ensure long-term recovery and improved quality of life.
At Pasunooti Neuro and Multispeciality Care, we are committed to breaking the stigma around mental health and providing compassionate, confidential, and evidence-based care for all.
