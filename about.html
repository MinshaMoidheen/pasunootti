Pasunooti Neuro and Multispeciality Care

Welcome to Pasunooti Neuro and Multispeciality Care, where advanced medical technology meets compassionate care. We offer a broad range of healthcare services, ensuring precise diagnosis and effective treatment across multiple specialities

At Pasunooti Neuro and Multispeciality Care, we are dedicated to delivering comprehensive, patient-centered healthcare with excellence and compassion. With a team of highly skilled specialists, we provide cutting-edge treatments across multiple medical disciplines, including General Medicine, Orthopedics, Neurosurgery, Physiotherapy, Psychiatry, and Diagnostics.

Our Mission

To provide patient-centered, cutting-edge, and ethical healthcare services, ensuring quality care is assesible to all.

Our Vision

To be the leading neuro and multispeciality healthcare provider, enhancing patient well-being through advanced medical care and holistic recovery.

Core Values

Excellence

Chairman's Message

At Pasunooti Neuro and Multispeciality Care, we are driven by a single goal-delivering world-class healthcare with compassion. Our commitment is to ensure that every patient recieves the highest level of care, combining state of the art technology with a personal touch. We believe in making quality healthcare accesible and affordable for all sections of society.

Director's Message
